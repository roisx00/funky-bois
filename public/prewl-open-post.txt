▌ NOTES — what's accurate vs the previous draft

The previous version claimed "every approved holder earns a guaranteed
mint." That's wrong. Approval flow is two-stage:

  1. APPROVED   → drop_eligible = TRUE → can claim traits every 5h
                                          → Tier 2 (FCFS) at mint
  2. APPROVED + PORTRAIT BUILT → is_whitelisted = TRUE → Tier 1 (GTD)
                                                       → guaranteed mint

Only Tier 1 gets the guaranteed seat. The new copy makes that distinction
explicit: "build a portrait, earn a guaranteed seat. don't build, you
still mint in the open round if you make it."
