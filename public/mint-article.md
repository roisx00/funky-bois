## Tier Eligibility

The pre-mint architecture is a two-stage funnel. Both tiers require a wallet
bound to the dashboard before mint day. Without a bound wallet, the seat
exists but cannot mint.

### Tier 1, Guaranteed

Tier 1 is the rewarded path. Holders who applied to the pre-whitelist,
passed manual admin review, claimed traits across drop sessions, and
**composed a finished portrait** are written into Tier 1.

**Requirements**
- Pre-whitelist approved
- Trait inventory accumulated
- Portrait composed and submitted
- Wallet bound on the dashboard

If you built a portrait but have not yet bound a wallet, that path is:
**Dashboard, Overview, Connect Wallet.**

### Tier 2, First Come First Served

Tier 2 holds seats for users who were pre-whitelist approved and gained
drop access, but did not finish a portrait before mint day. The mint
price is identical to Tier 1, the window is competitive instead of
guaranteed.

**Requirements**
- Pre-whitelist approved
- Drop access granted
- Wallet bound on the dashboard

If you are approved and did not build, this is your path:
**Dashboard, Overview, Connect Wallet.**

### Public

The fourth stage opens to anyone with the residual supply. 0.005 ETH,
ten per wallet, while supply lasts.

## What Comes After

The mint is a checkpoint, not a destination.

**The Vault evolves.** What runs off-chain today moves on-chain after
mint. The same state machine, the same upgrade tracks, the same
permanence. Genesis is the off-chain ledger snapshot. Every BUSTS,
every upgrade tier, every recorded burn carries forward unchanged.

**The games.** Vaults become the unit of competition across a sequence
of seasonal modes. Eight upgrade tracks, three tiers each, permanent
reinforcement. Win conditions multiply rewards and increment Power.
Loss conditions decay Power, but the Vault is never destroyed. The
mode catalog is intentionally not enumerated. The first release follows
the mint within weeks.

**$BUSTS.** The off-chain economy is staged for a transition. The
shape of the next form is documented in §10 of the litepaper but
deliberately not announced here. Holders should expect the system they
used pre-mint to take a more durable form. Nothing is reset. Nothing
is rebought.

**The reveal.** Tokens are sealed at mint with a placeholder. The full
1,969 portraits unlock together when the assembly is complete. No
staggered windows, no trait-sniping during open mint. One shared moment.
